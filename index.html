Welcome to Jitters


Jitters is a is simple Caffeine measurment tool for looking after your own daily caffeine intake.


To begin, set your daily limit.


After that simply press the + button to add more beverages to your daily count.


Every day when you load Jitters it will reset and you will be able to set your daily limit for how many Mg you want to intake in a day.


Leave the page open for the whole day as when you refresh it will reset your data.
